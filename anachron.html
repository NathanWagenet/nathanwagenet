A little about Anachron

Tom expects to ace his grad school comps—after all, he’s better at history than just about anyone—but there’s a lot he doesn’t know yet. Like, why time police from the future would take him with them on a chase into the past. And why do only he and the team’s second-in-command black out when they shift between eras?

Turns out that time travel is a relatively new science, and the scientists are still working out the kinks. In fact, certain parts of it aren’t lining up at all, and suddenly it’s up to Tom to figure out what’s really happening and stop the brilliant but secretive William Grey before he escapes into the past.

Tom is damn good with history. Too bad he isn’t a chronophysicist.

At least, not yet.
