A little about Rellan

Three of the Jewels of Unity have been found, but the Sapphire Necklace is still missing. Does Nirallan have it? Or was it found by another, years ago? One thing is certain: Llyren and the others must find it before they can confront and defeat Nirallan.

At least, that's what they think. But as they search for a lead, they find hints of a fifth temple, hidden among the impassable reefs and currents in the center of the three islands of Rellania, on a place called Calithnir, Tempest Rock.

Impassable, that is, without Shaping. And if they can reach it, they may just find a way to unite their Shaping and defeat Nirallan even without all four Jewels.
