A little about Kurt

The Sapphire Necklace is missing from the Temple of Water, so Llyren and friends set off for Barrochland to find the Onyx Bracelet, hoping that the shadowy Nirallan doesn't reach it first.

Kurt, however, is still smarting from Llyren's Shaping success in the battle with the ifrit. Kurt is used to being the strongest, the rock on which everyone else relies, but now he questions his identity and his purpose in the group.

As they journey deep into the heart of Barrochland, the land of his birth, Kurt hopes that he can discover what it means to truly be an Earthcarver.
