A little about Lis

Kurt has recovered the Onyx Bracelet from the Temple of Earth and is just beginning to learn how to use it, but the four Shapers can't rest yet. Next they must seek out the Emerald Circlet on the long island of Noskenesta.

Lis is hesitant to return home, since he left under less-than-ideal circumstances. He returns as a Shaper, true, but danger also nips at his heels, and he has never been brave, even under less dire conditions.

Now, when Kurt is captured along with the Onyx Bracelet, it falls on Lis to lead Llyren and Elraen deep into the Snowspine that only he knows and find both Kurt and the Temple of Air before it is too late.
